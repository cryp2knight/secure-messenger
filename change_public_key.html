To generate your keys, execute the following commands in your computer's command prompt or terminal.

openssl genrsa -out rsa_2048_priv.pem 2048
openssl rsa -pubout -in rsa_2048_priv.pem -out rsa_2048_pub.pem

or you can visit this page to generate your keys (if you are on mobile)

Note: Keep and secure your private key. You will not be able to decrypt and read your messages without your private key.
Also, pls USE 2048 KEY SIZE or HIGHER!!!
